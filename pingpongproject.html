Arduino Ping Pong Project

The ping pong project was one of the projects where I was able to work with a partner. Together me and my partner came up with an idea to build a catapult inorder to catapult the ping pong ball over the net. We built our machine using popsicle sticks and glue. For the arduino part of the project, we used servo motors, which was connected to a straw, to move the actual catapult. Overall, this project was a success and it was able to catapult the ball over the net.
